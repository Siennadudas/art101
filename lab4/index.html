The Best Blueberry Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 1.5 cups almond milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • a pinch of salt

Step by Step Instructions:

  • 1. Beat the egg in a large bowl. Add almond milk, vanilla extract, and melted butter and whisk until combined.
  • 2. Add the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Whisk until batter is smooth and evenly combined.
  • 3. Mix in your blueberries.
  • 4. Heat up a large pan to medium heat and coat evenly with butter.
  • 5. Use 1/4 measuring cup to scoop batter onto preheated pan. Cook pancakes 1-2 minutes on each side.
  • 6. Serve with buter and maple syrup, enjoy!
